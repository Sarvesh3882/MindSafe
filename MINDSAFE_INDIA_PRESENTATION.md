## SLIDE 4: Core Innovation - ARIA Assessment Engine

### Title
**ARIA: Adaptive Response & Insight Algorithm**

### Main Content

#### What Makes ARIA Different?
**Traditional Approach:**
❌ Clinical questionnaires (PHQ-9, GAD-7)  
❌ Students know they're being tested  
❌ Stigma and avoidance  
❌ Low completion rates  

**ARIA Approach:**
✅ Invisible assessment through daily check-ins  
✅ Feels like a wellness companion  
✅ Gamified with streaks and progress  
✅ 85%+ daily engagement  

### How ARIA Works (Flow Diagram)

```
Step 1: Emotion Tap
"How are you feeling today?"
[6 emoji tiles: 😊 😐 😔 😟 😰 😡]
         ↓
Step 2: Smart Follow-up
2-3 contextual questions
(Based on emotion selected)
         ↓
Step 3: Silent Scoring
Maps to PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS-10
User sees nothing clinical
         ↓
Step 4: Risk Classification
🟢 Stable | 🟠 Attention | 🔴 Critical
         ↓
Step 5: Action
Wellness tips | Counsellor suggestion | Instant alert
```

### Key Metrics Box
**📊 ARIA Performance:**
- **85+ questions** from validated instruments
- **7 categories** tracked (depression, anxiety, stress, sleep, burnout, loneliness, substance)
- **< 2 minutes** average completion time
- **Zero API cost** (hardcoded logic, not AI)

## 📝 PRESENTATION NOTES

### Design Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Teal (#14B8A6)
- Secondary: Purple (#A855F7)
- Accent: Blue (#3B82F6)
- Success: Green (#10B981)
- Warning: Orange (#F59E0B)
- Danger: Red (#EF4444)
- Background: Light gray (#F8FAF9)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, 32-40pt
- Subheadings: Semi-bold, 24-28pt
- Body: Regular, 16-20pt
- Use sans-serif fonts (Inter, Poppins, or Roboto)

**Layout:**
- Consistent margins (1 inch all sides)
- White space is your friend
- Use icons and visuals liberally
- Limit text per slide (bullet points, not paragraphs)

### Presentation Tips

**Slide 1 (Title):**
- Keep it clean and professional
- Logo should be prominent
- Set the tone with visuals

**Slide 2 (Problem):**
- Start with pain points (emotional connection)
- Transition to opportunity (excitement)
- Use statistics to build credibility

**Slide 3 (Solution):**
- Show the complete ecosystem
- Emphasize "one platform, three users"
- Use screenshots if available

**Slide 4 (ARIA):**
- This is your innovation differentiator
- Explain the "invisible" concept clearly
- Use the flow diagram to show simplicity

**Slide 5 (Technical):**
- Show you're production-ready
- Balance technical depth with accessibility
- Highlight real-time features

**Slide 6 (Business):**
- Clear pricing builds trust
- ROI justifies the investment
- End with strong CTA
