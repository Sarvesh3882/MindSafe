# Resources Section - Quick Guide 🎯

## For Counsellors

### Create a Resource
1. Click **"Create Resource"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   ```
   Resource Name: "5-Minute Breathing Exercise"
   Tips: "Practice this daily to reduce anxiety..."
   Link Type: [Video] or [Document]
   URL: https://youtube.com/... (optional)
   ```
3. Click **"Create Resource"**

### Prescribe to Student
1. Find your resource in the list
2. Click **"Prescribe to Student"**
3. Select student from dropdown
4. Done! ✅

---

## For Students

### View Your Resources
1. Go to **Resources** page
2. See all resources prescribed by your counsellor
3. Each resource shows:
   - 📝 Resource name
   - 👤 Prescribed by (counsellor name)
   - 📅 Date prescribed
   - 💡 Tips from counsellor
   - 🔗 Link to video/document

### Access Resource
1. Read the tips from your counsellor
2. Click **"View Video"** or **"View Document"**
3. Follow the guidance provided

**Counsellor Creates:**
```
Name: "Stress Management Techniques"
Tips: "These techniques will help you manage exam stress. 
       Practice the breathing exercise twice daily - 
       once in the morning and once before bed."
Type: Video
URL: https://youtube.com/watch?v=example
```

```
👤 Prescribed by Dr. Sharma
📅 May 17, 2026
```
