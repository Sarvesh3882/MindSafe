SLIDE 4: ARIA ASSESSMENT ENGINE
--------------------------------
ARIA: Adaptive Response & Insight Algorithm

TRADITIONAL APPROACH:
❌ Clinical questionnaires (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
❌ Students know they're being tested
❌ Stigma and avoidance
❌ Low completion rates

ARIA APPROACH:
✅ Invisible assessment through daily check-ins
✅ Feels like a wellness companion
✅ Gamified with streaks and progress
✅ 85%+ daily engagement

HOW ARIA WORKS:
Step 1: Emotion Tap → "How are you feeling today?" [6 emoji tiles]
Step 2: Smart Follow-up → 2-3 contextual questions
Step 3: Silent Scoring → Maps to PHQ-9, GAD-7, PSS-10 (user sees nothing clinical)
Step 4: Risk Classification → Green (Stable) | Orange (Attention) | Red (Critical)
Step 5: Action → Wellness tips | Counsellor suggestion | Instant alert

ARIA PERFORMANCE:
• 85+ questions from validated instruments
• 7 categories tracked (depression, anxiety, stress, sleep, burnout, loneliness, substance)
• < 2 minutes average completion time
• Zero API cost (hardcoded logic, not AI)

"Clinical accuracy without clinical feel — that's ARIA"
